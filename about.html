Canada is a welcoming country for foreigners who wish to come to study, work or immigrate. Citizenship and Immigration Services Canada (CIC) is responsible for managing immigration, citizenship, study and work programs for foreign nationals wishing to come to Canada. Study programs allow foreign students to come and study at Canadian educational institutions, while work programs allow skilled foreign workers to work in Canada for a fixed term. Immigration programs are designed to enable qualified immigrants to come and settle in Canada on a permanent basis. CICs are responsible for ensuring processes are fair and transparent for all applicants and work closely with Canadian employers to meet labor market needs and help drive the country's economic growth. Canada also offers a naturalization process to become a Canadian citizen, allowing permanent residents to become full members of Canadian society.
